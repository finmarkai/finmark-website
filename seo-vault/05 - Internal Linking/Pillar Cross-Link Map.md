## Cross-link rationale

### P1 вҖ” AI Financial Automation
| Links to | Why |
|---|---|
| **P2 Compliance** | Compliance is a category of automation; broad pillar overlap |
| **P5 Reporting** | Reporting is the most-visible AI automation use case |
| **P8 Workflow** | Workflow is the deepest related topic вҖ” same audience |

### P2 вҖ” Compliance Automation
| Links to | Why |
|---|---|
| **P3 Fraud Detection** | KYC/AML overlap with fraud вҖ” same data, same buyers |
| **P4 Payments** | Payment compliance is half of compliance work |
| **P5 Reporting** | Regulatory reporting is part of both pillars |

### P3 вҖ” Fraud Detection & Risk
| Links to | Why |
|---|---|
| **P2 Compliance** | KYC/AML overlap |
| **P4 Payments** | Payment fraud is the most common fraud type |
| **P6 Data Platform** | Fraud detection needs cross-source unified data |

### P4 вҖ” Payment Processing
| Links to | Why |
|---|---|
| **P3 Fraud Detection** | Built-in fraud screening for payments |
| **P2 Compliance** | Cross-border payment compliance |
| **P8 Workflow** | P2P (procure-to-pay) and O2C (order-to-cash) flows |

### P5 вҖ” Financial Reporting
| Links to | Why |
|---|---|
| **P7 Forecasting** | Reporting feeds forecasting (and vice versa) |
| **P6 Data Platform** | Reports need unified data |
| **P1 AI Fin Automation** | Reporting is the brand-defining pillar's most visible output |

### P6 вҖ” Data Platform
| Links to | Why |
|---|---|
| **P5 Reporting** | Data вҶ’ reports |
| **P7 Forecasting** | Data вҶ’ forecasts |
| **P3 Fraud** | Cross-source data is critical for fraud detection |

### P7 вҖ” Predictive Forecasting
| Links to | Why |
|---|---|
| **P5 Reporting** | Forecasting needs reporting infrastructure |
| **P6 Data Platform** | Forecasting needs unified historical data |
| **P1 AI Fin Automation** | FP&A is a category of AI financial automation |

### P8 вҖ” Workflow Automation
| Links to | Why |
|---|---|
| **P1 AI Fin Automation** | Parent topic вҖ” workflow is automation in motion |
| **P4 Payments** | P2P/O2C touch payments directly |
| **P5 Reporting** | R2R (record-to-report) flows into reporting |

## Inbound link counts per pillar

Each pillar receives **3 sibling cross-links** + **N cluster links** + **1 homepage** + **N solution links** + **N blog links**.

Approximate inbound link count per pillar (post Phase 4):
- 3 (siblings) + 8-9 (clusters Г— 2 each) + 1 (homepage) + 3 (solutions) + 5 (blog) вүҲ **30+ inbound links per pillar**

That's the equity concentration that lets pillars rank for high-difficulty terms.
