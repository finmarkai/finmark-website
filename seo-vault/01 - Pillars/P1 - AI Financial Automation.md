## Why this pillar exists

FinMark.ai's homepage tagline is "The Future of Financial and Operational Automation/Intelligence/Analytics/Compliance." The phrase "AI financial automation" is the most direct match for that promise and the broadest TOFU funnel entry.

This pillar should:
1. Define what AI financial automation IS (in the same way HubSpot defined "inbound marketing")
2. Enumerate the categories it covers (compliance, fraud, payments, reporting, etc.) — each linking to its own pillar
3. Tell the FinMark.ai story
4. Funnel readers to a demo
