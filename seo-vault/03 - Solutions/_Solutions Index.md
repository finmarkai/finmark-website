## Linking pattern for solution pages

Solution pages are **hubs**, not destinations. They link to multiple pillars relevant to the persona/industry.

For example, a Banks page links to:
- [[P2 - Compliance Automation]] (banks live and die by compliance)
- [[P3 - AI Fraud Detection]] (banks need fraud)
- [[P4 - Payment Processing Automation]] (core banking)
- [[P5 - AI Financial Reporting]] (regulatory reporting)
- [[P6 - Unified Financial Data Platform]] (core systems integration)

A CFO persona page links to:
- All 8 pillars (because CFOs care about everything)
- But emphasizes [[P5 - AI Financial Reporting]] and [[P7 - Predictive Financial Forecasting]] above the fold

## Page structure (template)

1. **Hero** — persona/industry-specific headline
2. **Pain points** specific to the audience
3. **How FinMark.ai solves them** — link to 4-6 relevant pillars
4. **Use cases** — concrete examples
5. **Customer story** — link to a case study
6. **CTA** → `/demo`
