# 3-Way Matching (NAV)

> **Parent pillar:** [[Accounts Payable Automation]]
> **Primary keyword:** `3 way matching nav`
> **URL:** `/accounts-payable-automation/3-way-matching`

## Page intent
Cover automated 3-way matching specifically against Microsoft Dynamics NAV PO/GRN data. Capture both the generic "3-way matching" search and the more specific "3-way matching NAV" buyer query. Highlights lump-sum and ratio-based matching as real production differentiators.

## Real product specifics
- **PO/GRN sync from NAV every 5 minutes** (via Ahmed's staging API)
- **9,589+ PO lines, 1,127+ GRN lines** synced for TGID alone
- **Configurable tolerances** per company/vendor/category
- **Lump-sum matching** for telecom invoices (1 PO line + N invoice lines)
- **Ratio-based matching** for marketing invoices (split by PO line amounts)
- 2-way matching also supported for services and recurring charges
